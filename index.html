The problem

Life is not organized around single-purpose apps.

A wedding app handles one part of a wedding. A home app handles one device ecosystem. A list app tracks one set of tasks. The customer ends up connecting everything manually.

Savor & Simplify is built around the real way people live: events, homes, vendors, purchases, deadlines, reminders, documents, and alerts all overlapping at once.
