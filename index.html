The problem

The brand is bigger than one app.

A wedding app handles one part of a wedding. A home app handles one device ecosystem. A list app tracks one set of tasks. The customer ends up connecting everything manually.

Savor & Simplify is the umbrella for solutions that reduce that burden. Some offerings may be apps. Some may be templates, dashboards, monitoring, concierge setup, or advisory services. The common thread is helping people simplify real life.
